About Me

Hi, I'm Sophie

I'm a natural light photographer based in South Africa, and I have an absolute passion for capturing authentic, beautiful moments.

Whether it's your wedding day, a cozy couple shoot, or a joyful family session — I'll make sure you feel completely at ease, have a blast, and walk away with images you'll treasure forever.

Think of our shoot as a fun hangout where we happen to snap some stunning pictures. No awkward poses, no stiff smiles — just real, candid moments filled with love and laughter.
