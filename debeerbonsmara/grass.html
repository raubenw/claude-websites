Planting Guide

01

Prepare the Soil

Test soil pH (ideal: 6.0-7.0). Till to 4-6 inches depth. Remove debris and weeds.

02

Choose the Right Time

Summer mix: April-June. Winter mix: Sept-Nov. Avoid planting before heavy rain.

03

Broadcast & Roll

Spread seeds evenly at 15-20 lbs/acre. Lightly roll or drag to ensure seed-soil contact.

04

Water & Wait

Keep soil moist for 2-3 weeks. Avoid grazing until grass reaches 6-8 inches.
